## About Stay Nivas

In Sanskrit, *nivas* means dwelling — not just a structure, but the feeling of being held by a place.

Stay Nivas is a hospitality journal. We review boutique and design-forward hotels through the lens of experience: how a property makes you feel, what it's trying to say, and whether it earns your return.

The perspective is shaped by two traditions of hospitality. The Indian one — *atithi devo bhava*, the guest is god — where hosting is sacred, anticipatory, and deeply personal. And the Western design tradition, where space, light, and intention create meaning without a word being spoken.

We believe the best properties live at the intersection: thoughtful design and genuine warmth. Places where someone decided what you should feel before you arrived, and the staff makes sure you actually feel it.

Every review is a single stay, written with care. No star ratings, no affiliate links, no listicles. Just one property at a time, explored with the attention it deserves.
