# Stay Nivas — Brand Style Guide

## Brand Identity

**Name:** Stay Nivas
**Domain:** staynivas.com
**Tagline:** *Where you dwell matters.*

**Nivas** (निवास) — Sanskrit for "dwelling" or "abode." Not just a place to sleep, but a place that holds you. Stay Nivas reviews boutique and luxury properties through the lens of feeling, presence, and cultural depth — shaped by an Indian-American perspective on what true hospitality means.

---

## Brand Positioning

Stay Nivas is not a travel blog. It is a hospitality journal.

We review properties the way a thoughtful guest experiences them — not as a checklist of amenities but as an unfolding encounter with a place. We care about how a lobby changes your breathing, why a designer chose that particular stone, and whether the staff remembers your name or your preferences.

**Core lens:** East meets West hospitality. The Indian tradition of *atithi devo bhava* (the guest is god) as an intuitive framework for evaluating how properties welcome, hold, and release their guests.

**We are for:** General managers, hospitality professionals, and discerning travelers who care about the *why* behind a property — not just the *what*.

**We are not:** A deals site, a points-and-miles blog, a listicle factory, or an influencer page.

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary | Charcoal | `#2C2C2C` | Body text, headers |
| Secondary | Warm Sand | `#D4C5A9` | Backgrounds, section dividers |
| Accent | Deep Saffron | `#C1571B` | Links, highlights, pull quotes |
| Neutral Light | Ivory | `#F5F0E8` | Page background |
| Neutral Dark | Slate | `#4A4A4A` | Secondary text, captions |

### Typography

- **Headlines:** Freight Display (or similar editorial serif — alternatives: Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond). Used for property names, article titles, and pull quotes.
- **Body:** Inter or Source Sans Pro. Clean, highly readable sans-serif. Generous line height (1.6–1.75).
- **Accent/Captions:** Same sans-serif, lighter weight, slightly smaller. Used for photo captions, metadata, dates.

### Typography Hierarchy

```
H1 — Property Name / Article Title (Freight Display, 36–48px)
H2 — Section Headers (Freight Display, 24–28px)
H3 — Subsection (Sans-serif, bold, 18–20px)
Body — Sans-serif, 16–17px, #2C2C2C on #F5F0E8
Caption — Sans-serif, 13–14px, #4A4A4A
```

### Photography Direction

- **Natural light only.** No flash, no heavy processing.
- **Atmospheric over informational.** We want the viewer to feel the space, not inventory it.
- **Detail-forward.** Textures, materials, craftsmanship. The weave of a throw, the grain of a wooden door, the pour of a cup of chai.
- **People are secondary.** When present, candid and mid-experience — never posing, never looking at camera.
- **No collages, no grids, no before/after.** One image at a time, given room to breathe.

### Layout Principles

- **Generous white space.** Let the content and photography breathe. The site should feel unhurried.
- **Single-column for reviews.** No sidebars cluttering the reading experience.
- **Full-bleed images** between text sections. Photos earn their own space.
- **Minimal navigation.** Home, Reviews, About. That's it until there's a reason for more.

### Logo Direction

- Wordmark only. No icon/symbol needed.
- "Stay Nivas" in the headline serif, with "Stay" in regular weight and "Nivas" in bold or semi-bold.
- Deep saffron or charcoal on light backgrounds. Ivory on dark.
- No tagline in the logo — it stands alone.

## Brand Voice (Summary)

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Write like a thoughtful guest | Write like a reviewer with a clipboard |
| Lead with feeling, follow with detail | Lead with specs and star ratings |
| Be specific and sensory | Be vague and superlative ("amazing," "stunning") |
| Reference culture and craft | Name-drop brands for their own sake |
| Have quiet confidence | Oversell or gush |
| Use "we" naturally when traveling with family | Perform family content |

## File & Asset Naming

- Property images: `{property-slug}/{descriptor}.jpg` (e.g., `alila-villas-uluwatu/arrival-pavilion.jpg`)
- Review posts: `{YYYY-MM-DD}-{property-slug}.md`
- All lowercase, hyphens, no spaces.
